About Lesotho Hub

Lesotho Hub is a digital platform created to empower livestock, poultry, and crop farmers across Lesotho. Our goal is to provide a reliable online marketplace where farmers can advertise their products, connect with buyers, and strengthen agricultural trade in the country. We bring together small-scale farmers, agribusinesses, and consumers to create a stronger agricultural economy that benefits everyone.

Agriculture is the backbone of Lesothoâ€™s economy, and through this hub, we aim to bridge the gap between farmers and consumers by offering a transparent and user-friendly space for business growth. Beyond connecting markets, we also promote capacity building by sharing resources, modern farming techniques, and innovative solutions to challenges faced by farmers. This ensures that both traditional and young farmers are included in shaping the future of agriculture in Lesotho.

Our Objectives

  • Promote locally produced livestock, poultry, and crop products.
  • Enable farmers to reach more customers through digital access.
  • Encourage fair pricing and transparency in agricultural markets.
  • Provide a platform for young entrepreneurs in farming.
  • Support food security and sustainable farming practices in Lesotho.
